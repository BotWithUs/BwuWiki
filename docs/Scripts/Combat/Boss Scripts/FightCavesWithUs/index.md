## Features

:::

<ContentBlock title="Features">

> - **Flexible Combat**: No hard-coded requirements - works with your available gear (T80+ recommended, T90 optimal)
> - **Priority Targeting**: Target selection based on priority
> - **Combat Style**: Revolution combat support (Necromancy recommended due to conjure sustain)
> - **Banking Options**: War's Retreat or Max Guild banking
> - **Aura System**: Queue-based aura rotation with automatic reset functionality
> - **Scripture Support**: Ful, Wen, Jas, Grimoire with automatic refilling
> - **Prayer Options**: Soul Split or protect/deflect prayers
> - **Consumables**: Prayer/Restore potions, Overloads, Incense sticks, Powders, Excalibur usage
> - **Death Handling**: Automatic recovery and continuation
> - **Flexible Start**: Begin at War's Retreat or Max Guild with portal configured for Fight Caves

</ContentBlock>
